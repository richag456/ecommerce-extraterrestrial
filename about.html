About Us

Extraterrestrial is here to simplify your interplanetary excursions. With so many spaceports, destinations, and the sheer amount of options and information that comes along with space travel, planning an out of this world trip can be stressful and confusing. Our goal is to uncomplicate your desires and needs and to help you enjoy the beauty of space burden-free.
